About Greenland Mining Capital

Independent equity research on publicly traded companies in the Greenland and broader Arctic mining sector. Our coverage focuses on PGM producers, developers, and explorers with an emphasis on resource quality, jurisdictional risk, and development catalysts.

Previously covered mining equities at [redacted] Capital Management and [redacted] Securities. Focus on small-cap developers with tier-1 resource assets in favorable jurisdictions.

Methodology

Our analysis uses a multi-factor framework incorporating: (1) NI 43-101 / JORC compliant resource data, (2) jurisdictional risk scoring based on Fraser Institute rankings and regulatory environment, (3) development stage and timeline assessment, (4) peer-relative valuation using P/NAV, EV/Resource oz, and EV/EBITDA comparables, (5) catalyst identification and timing analysis.

Data Sources

  • NI 43-101 Technical Reports and Feasibility Studies
  • SEC Filings (10-K, 10-Q, S-1)
  • Corporate investor presentations and press releases
  • USGS Mineral Commodity Summaries
  • TradingView market data
  • Fraser Institute Annual Mining Survey
